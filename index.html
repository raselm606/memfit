About Memfit

MemFit is community. The community for all levels of the health and wellbeing industry. Find, engage and relate in a simple, fast, intuitive, application that helps everyone achieve their goals - The Member, Trainer and Centre. Our mission is “to take the position of the most trusted, authentic, and connected platform for the entire health and wellbeing industry”
We are on a journey, come with us.

Member

You are now the owner of your profile, transferable across your entire health and wellbeing journey.
  • You own and create your goals and objectives, weight, height, profile, key interest areas’
  • You control your preferred payment method/s (no more cash, clunky card payments!)
  • You can have multiple centres, managers’/trainers, as you probably already do!
  • You retain your own profile and data – It isn’t locked away in a filing cabinet somewhere! Your profile comes with you on your health and wellbeing journey.
  • You can review your trainer, centre and sessions, and provide realtime data to the centre/trainer. No more broken equipment, late sessions, etc.!
